Hand-Carved Rosewood Dining Table

Project Vision

A bespoke dining piece designed to seat ten, celebrating the natural imperfections and deep, rich tones of sustainably sourced Indian Rosewood. The request involves subtle, geometric hand-carving along the apron, merging contemporary minimalist lines with traditional Jaipur woodworking techniques.

Dimensions specified are 120" x 42" x 30", with a matte, hand-rubbed oil finish to maintain the tactile quality of the timber over generations.
