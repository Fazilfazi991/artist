The Artisan Behind the Wheel

Maya Studio was born from a desire to bridge the gap between ancient Indian pottery techniques and contemporary design. Trained by master artisans in Rajasthan and refined by modern design sensibilities, Maya creates pieces that are both functional objects and sculptural art.

Every piece is hand-thrown, glazed, and fired in our small Jaipur workshop, ensuring no two items are exactly alike. We believe in the beauty of imperfection and the slow rhythm of handmade goods.
