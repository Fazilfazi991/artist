Story expand_more

Hand-thrown on a traditional potter's wheel in the heart of Jaipur, the Earthen Terra Vase embodies the raw, tactile beauty of local clay. Its minimalist, organic silhouette is inspired by ancient Indian storage vessels, reimagined for the modern home. Left unglazed to highlight the natural texture and subtle tonal shifts of the fired earth.

Craft Process expand_more

Sourced locally from the riverbeds of Rajasthan, the clay is refined and aged before being shaped. Each piece is pit-fired using traditional methods, resulting in unique scorch marks and subtle color variations. No two vases are exactly alike.
